    What is Boss Mode Sudoku?

    Boss Mode Sudoku is an online Sudoku game that can instantly switch into a professional fake dashboard. Perfect for office breaks, study pauses and quick brain training.

    How Boss Mode Works

    Move your mouse left and right several times or click the Boss Mode button. The Sudoku board disappears and a realistic analytics dashboard appears instantly.

    Why Play Sudoku Daily?

    Sudoku improves memory, concentration, logical reasoning and mental discipline. Playing a few minutes each day is a powerful habit for your brain.
